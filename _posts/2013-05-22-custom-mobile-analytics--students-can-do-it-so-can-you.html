Have you ever wondered how to create a mobile analytics app using data from Salesforce? What about how to push your mobile app data into Salesforce? Sure, you may be an expert with Salesforce’s platform, but how do you integrate it with the mobile platforms out there?A group of senior Software Engineering students from Cal Poly University took on this challenge during this year’s Software Engineering Capstone class taught by Professor David Janzen. Every year the students take on projects from industry clients.
