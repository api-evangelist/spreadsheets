In Part 1 and Part 2, we talked about open-source projects that started outside of Salesforce.But, does Salesforce create and contribute new open source releases as well? Historically, yes, although typically not core platform features… But, this is changing. With over 4 million custom apps written on our Platform, and 1.5 million developers in our community, the importance of open sourcing core platform technologies is critical for continued success.
