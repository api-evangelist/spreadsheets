A lot of times companies wish there was an enterprise version of some cool consumer app so that they can use it privately within their organization.For example, virtually every company organizes events for their employees and customers on a regular basis.They may want an iOS photo-sharing app that lists photos taken at those events, allow applying cool photo-filters and share them with their employees. Let’s see how we can leverage Salesforce1 Platform to build such photo sharing apps rather quickly.Salesforce1 mBaaS Platform Salesforce1 Platform, among other things, is also a secure and world-class Mobile Backend as a service (mBaaS) platform that allows us to build any mobile app we want.
