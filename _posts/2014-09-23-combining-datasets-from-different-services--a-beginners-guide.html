Much like how DJs combine and remix existing songs to create something new, analysts today are combining datasets from different sources to make something new.These new data combinations offer new insights that the original datasets in their silos couldn’t offer on their own.Most business professionals keep their data stored in many different places.For example, they have email marketing data from MailChimp, sales data in Salesforce, revenue data in Stripe, and even Excel files on their computer.With so much data kept everywhere, it’s hard to put the entire picture together.
