Weve got some great news for folks who maintain their BOMs in Excel!Now you can easily pull pricing, availability, Buy Now URLs, datasheet URLs, and more right into your spreadsheet with a few simple functions.The first thing you need to do is download and install DataNitro into Excel.DataNitro is a plugin that allows you to use Python functions within Excel.The installation is a very straightforward process.
