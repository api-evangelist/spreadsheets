We’ve seen you build impressive analyses with data from kimono. To make analyzing your data even easier, we’ve built an add-on that lets you import data from your kimono APIs directly into google spreadsheets.Use kimono with google spreadsheets to: What story does your data tell?We’ve seen correlations between bitcoin and other market indicators, comparative analytics of popular growth hacker articles, an in-depth investigation into the highs and lows of Nicholas Cage’s movie career, and a test to see if Malcolm Gladwell’s theory on athlete birth dates applies to football player birth dates and much more.We’re excited to see what you will build with kimono!
