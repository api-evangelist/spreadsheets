With the Spring’14 release the Salesforce Analytics API allows users to dynamically choose date and get historical data.To understand how we can take advantage of this enhancement, lets take a look at the following screenshot.What’s the Problem?Imagine you are a sales manager who wants to look at the open pipeline for an account and compare it to what it was at the beginning of the week or even beginning of the month.To achieve this in any traditional business system it would require access to the data-warehousing system — a time-consuming and expensive process.
